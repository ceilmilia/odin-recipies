Sizzle McTizzle

Description

This is a wonderfuly hoooot recipe that contains every pepper in existance and some we invented just for this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Love
  • 1 Cup Ghost Peppers
  • 1 Cup Olive Oil
  • 1 Cup Habenero Peppers
  • Spice to taste
  • 12oz Coconut Milk
  • Red Curry Paste

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees.
  2. Mix together dry inredients in a small bowl.
  3. Mix Wet ingredients together with a stand mixer.
  4. Add Dry to wet slowly and mix it in throughly.
  5. Chop up the remaing bits of love and mince it across the top.
  6. Pop it into the oven 35 mins.
