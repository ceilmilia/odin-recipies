Snazzy Sugar Poptarts

Tired of the same two flavors your kids have to have? Don't look any further for #freedom from highly processed breakfeast sugar pasteries because we have got you covered with a surefire recipe that will kill the craving for subpar cornsyrup pastry products.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Love
  • 1 Cup Ghost Peppers
  • 1 Cup Olive Oil
  • 1 Cup Habenero Peppers
  • Spice to taste
  • 12oz Coconut Milk
  • Red Curry Paste

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees.
  2. Mix together dry inredients in a small bowl.
  3. Mix Wet ingredients together with a stand mixer.
  4. Add Dry to wet slowly and mix it in throughly.
  5. Chop up the remaing bits of love and mince it across the top.
  6. Pop it into the oven 35 mins.
